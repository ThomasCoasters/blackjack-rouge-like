classic blackjack rules:
grab cards to get as close to 21 without going over.
use cards to help you reach 21.
if you go over 21, you bust!
new rules:
the bust number can change.
you have hands and discards.
your hands are the amount of times you can use your cards.
you can use your discards to remove a card from your hand.
use upgrades to change your playstyle:
after getting the required score, you can choose an upgrade.
upgrades can give you more hands, change the bust number, or give you special abilities!
