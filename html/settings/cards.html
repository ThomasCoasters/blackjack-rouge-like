card mechanics:
hover over cards to see their effects.
grabbing/using cards:
drag the bottom card in the corner to cards area to a new card.
drag it to the top to play all the held cards.
discarding cards:
click on the card to discard it.
discarding cards removes it from your hand but you lose a discard.
value and score:
each card has a value that adds to your total value.
don't go over the bust number!
after playing the cards the score is calculated.
value does not equal score.
