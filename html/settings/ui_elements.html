score and required score:
left top you can see the required score to win the round.
under that you can see your current score.
if your score is equal to or higher than the required score, you can choose an upgrade!
hands/discards:
left bottom you can see your hands and discards.
hands are used when you play all your cards.
discards are used when you discard a card from your hand.
blackjack bonus:
if you get exactly the same value as the bust value is with your cards, you get a blackjack bonus!
this multiplies your score by the amount of blackjack bonus you have.
you can see the current blackjack bonus under your discards.
cards amount:
above the deck cards you can see the amount of cards you have / full deck size.
